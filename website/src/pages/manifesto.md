## The Problem

AI systems today lock you in. Your conversations, your preferences, your context — trapped inside products you don't control. Cancel your subscription and everything that made the AI useful to you disappears. Switch providers and you start from zero.

This isn't a bug. It's the business model. The more context you give the AI, the harder it is to leave. Your own data becomes the wall that keeps you in.

## The Architecture

The Personal AI Architecture is a lock-in-free foundation for personal AI systems. Four components, two connectors, five principles — designed so that every piece is replaceable, including the architecture itself.

- **Your Memory** is the platform. Everything else is built on top, all replaceable.
- **The Engine** connects AI models to tools. New capabilities arrive by adding tools, not changing the engine.
- **Auth** protects your memory. Independent of everything else.
- **The Gateway** is how the world connects. Any client, any interface.
- **Two contracts** (Gateway API, Provider API) keep everything swappable via adapters.

## Why We're Giving This Away

We believe lock-in-free AI is imperative — for individuals, for economies, for societies. The architecture is published, tested (243 tests), and documented (13 specs). But it shouldn't be shaped by one team.

This is an invitation to a public architecture discussion. Read the specs. Challenge the principles. Build on it.
